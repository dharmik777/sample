About Me!

Web Developer/Designer

Hi, I'm Dharmik! Welcome to my portfolio. I'm a web developer/designer with a knack for creating fresh new websites for you and/or your company. My road to web developing has been a bit winding, and I definitely took a step or two off the beaten path but it all lead to this. I have a passion for the arts and actually got my MFA from Louisana State University; so, now I use that artistic passion, curiousity, and intrigue to excel as a web developer! Did I mention that I am also a freelance photographer? Well I am. I mostly do portraits and headshots, but shoot the occasional wedding, event or promotional material as well. Click on the "Portfolio" link to check out my work and then contact me if you'd like me to design your website and/or take the pictures that'll go on it!.
